about me

My name is YOHSUKE TAKAHASHI ,
I’m a Curator.

Yosuke Takahashi is a curator. Graduated from Tokyo University of the Arts, Department of Inter Media Art. After working as curator (chief curator) at 21st Century Museum of Contemporary Art, Kanazawa (2014-2021) and curator at Kadokawa Musashino Museum (2021-2022), he established decontext. Recent exhibitions include "Art in the Age of Super-replication Technology (GYRE, Omotesando, 2023/Damien Hirst and others), and "Liminalism" (pellas gallery, Boston, 2023/Yoichi Ochiai and others), an exhibition at the gallery of the Pellas family, an American noble family.
